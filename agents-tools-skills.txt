                 |
                 ├─> text2sql_query_generator_agent -> MCP: text2sql_query_context
                 |                                         text2sql_validate_sql
                 |                                         text2sql_data_context
                 |
                 ├─> text2sql_query_executor_agent  -> MCP: text2sql_query_executor
                 |
                 └─> text2sql_data_presenter_agent  -> formats & returns result to user
